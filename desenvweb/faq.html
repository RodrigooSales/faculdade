How do I create a playlist?
To create a playlist, go to your Library and click on the "Create Playlist" button. Give your playlist a name and description, then start adding your favorite songs to it.
Can I download songs for offline listening?
Yes, Spotify Premium users can download songs, albums, and playlists for offline listening. Just click the download button next to the content you want to save.
